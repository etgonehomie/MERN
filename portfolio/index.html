Who am I?

I am a self taught developer. Started off with Swift for iOS. However, I learned the MERN stack to broaden my skills and build cross-platform web applications and mobile apps. I love the feeling of finally squashing that bug and can build to meet the face-paced environment and changing requirements.

During my free time, I love to read, solve puzzles, play boardgames, and watch Survior. My favorite desinations are Disneyworld and Japan.
